About us

The FCIT Robotics Society is a student-led community dedicated to inducing a passion for robotics and technology among students at the Faculty of Computing and Information Technology (FCIT). We run workshops, compete in challenges, and collaborate on projects that combine hardware, software and creative problem-solving

Our Projects

Obstacle avoidance robot

A robot that uses ultrasonic sensors to detect and navigate around obstacles in its path.

Line following robot

A robot that can follow a line on the ground using infrared sensors to detect the path.

Robotic arm

A programmable robotic arm capable of performing tasks such as picking and placing objects using computer vision.
